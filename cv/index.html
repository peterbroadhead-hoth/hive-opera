    • Carole Hayman

    Carole Hayman is a Writer, Producer & Presenter. She was born in Kent, which has featured in her trilogy of novels, "The Warfleet Chronicles". Her early work was with The Bristol Old Vic, The Royal Court and Traverse Theatres, from which she went on to become a founder member and first woman director of the Joint Stock Theatre Company. She was an Associate Director of The Royal Court Theatre, for which she directed many World Premieres. Carole writes for radio, television and film. On radio, she is well known as writer of the Radio 4 hit series "Ladies of Letters" with Prunella Scales and Patricia Routledge, which has run for twelve series on radio and three series on ITV . She has written many other TV series and films, including The Refuge with Sue Townsend for Channel Four and Rides , the first ever all female TV drama, for BBC1.
    Most are available as books, audio books and DVDs.
